Sweet Potato Casserole

The Best Sweet Potato Casserole

This is the perfect fall recipe to bring to any family gathering or occasion as a side dish, or a dessert!

Topped with pecans, oats and marshmallows, this delicious treat is hard to resist!

Ingredients

Sweet Potatoes
  • 3 1/2 - 4 pounds sweet potatoes - peeled and cut into 1" pieces
  • 4 tbsp. (1/2 stick) unsalted butter
  • 3/4 cup packed light brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tsp. kosher salt
  • 1 tbsp. pure vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp. ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp. ground nutmeg
Topping
  • 2 cups mini marshmallows
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 4 tbsp. unsalted butter
  • 6 tbsp. packed light brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup old fashioned rolled oats
  • 1/4 tsp. ground cinnamon

Steps

  1. Add sweet potato pieces to large pot with water. Bring to a boil over high heat. Cook until fork tender.
  2. Drain potatoes and place back into pot. Begin mashing the potatoes with a potato masher, or blend with an electric mixer, until mostly smooth.
  3. Add in the butter, brown sugar, heavy cream, kosher salt, vanilla extract, cinnamon and nutmeg. Mix until combined
  4. Preheat oven to 350Â°F. Grease an 8"X10" casserole dish.
  5. Spoon sweet potatoes into casserole dish and smooth out. Top with marshmallows.
  6. Melt butter in a microwave safe bowl. Add flour, brown sugar, oats and cinnamon to the butter. Sprinkle over top of casserole.
  7. Bake for 20-25 minutes until streusel is crisp and marshmallows are golden brown.
