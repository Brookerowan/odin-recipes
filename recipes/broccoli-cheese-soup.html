Brocolli Cheese Soup

Delicious Broccoli Cheese Soup

This hearty soup is the perfect comfort food to warm your soul on a chilly fall day. Chances are, it will quickly become one of your staple fall and winter recipes.

This broccoli cheese soup is deliciously cheesy, amazingly rich and creamy and is loaded with fresh, homemade flavor and best of all, it's so easy to make!

Ingredients

  • 3 Cups(packed) finely chopped broccoli florets - you'll only need the florets for this recepe (the tree-like ends)
  • 6 Tbsp butter - cut into 1 Tbsp pieces
  • 1 Yellow onion - finely chopped
  • 1 Clove of garlic - finely minced
  • 1/4 Cup + 3 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/4 Cups milk
  • 14.5 oz Chicken broth
  • 1/2 Cup heavy cream
  • 2 1/2 Cups sharp cheddar cheese *for a bolder cheese flavor, consider using extra sharp cheddar
  • 1/3 Cup parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

    • Steps

    1. Saute onion:Melt your butter in a pot over medium-high heat. Add onions and cook, stirring frequently for about 4-5 minutes, or until onions are nearly soft.
    2. Make roux:Add garlic and flower to the pot and cook for about 1 minute, stirring constantly.
    3. Add liquids:While whisking, slowly pour in milk, followed by the chicken broth. Stir in broccoli.
    4. Let thicken:Cook until the mixture begins to thicken, stirring constantly.
    5. Let broccoli cook:Reduce heat to low and allow to cook for about 6 - 8 minutes, until broccoli is tender. Stir frequently
    6. Add cream and cheeses:Stir in heavy cream, cheddar and parmesan cheese. Mix until melted.
    7. Serve:Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve warm and feel free to top with cheddar.
